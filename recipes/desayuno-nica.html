Desayuno Nicarag√ºense (traditional Nicaraguan breakfast)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup cooked red or black beans (with a bit of broth)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ripe plantain
  • "Queso frito" (fried cheese) or "queso fresco" (fresh cheese)
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Optional: sour cream or "crema", avocado, coffee

Steps:

  1. Gallo Pinto

    2. Heat oil in a pan, mix the rice and beans together
    with a bit of bean broth, and cook until both beans and
    rice are slightly crispy and well mixed.

  2. Fry the Eggs

    3. Fry eggs to your preference (sunny side up is typical).

  3. Fry Plantains

    4. Slice plantains (approx. 1/8" thick slices) and fry in
    oil until golden and caramelized

    OPTIONAL: You may also use green plantains
    and lightly salt after frying for a
    more savory palate.

  4. Fry Cheese (optional):

    5. Fry thick slices of cheese until golden on the outside
    and soft on the inside.

    NOTE: you may opt to use a hard cheese
    like feta if you do not have access to a traditional
    Nicaraguan cheese like 'cuajada'.

  5. Assemble:

    6. Serve gallo pinto with fried eggs, plantains, cheese,
    and warm Tortillas. Add crema and avocado if desired.

Enjoy with a cup of bold Nicaraguan coffee!
